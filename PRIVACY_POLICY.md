# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 5, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Cozy Quest HD ("the Game") handles information when you use our Discord Activity.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information from Discord
When you launch the Game as a Discord Activity, we receive basic profile information provided by Discord, including your Discord user ID and username. This is used solely to identify you within the game session.

### Gameplay Data
We may collect gameplay data such as in-game actions and session information to facilitate the multiplayer experience. This data is temporary and exists only for the duration of a game session.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use the information collected to:
- Provide and operate the multiplayer game experience
- Identify players within a game session
- Maintain and improve the Game

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. The Game uses the following third-party services that may process data according to their own privacy policies:
- **Discord** — [Discord Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy)
- **Playroom** — Used for multiplayer functionality

## 5. Data Retention

Game session data is temporary and is not persisted after a session ends. We do not maintain long-term databases of player information.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed during gameplay. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

## 9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/zachlandes/cozy-quest-hd).
